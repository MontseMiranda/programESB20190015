Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa

This impressive Thai-style hotel is set in the Sierra Cortina Mountains, with magnificent views over the Costa Blanca. Surrounded by pine woods, it offers 7 outdoor pools and a spa. Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa, features stylish rooms with free WiFi. The design is inspired by Bali, and kimonos and slippers are included.

Thai massages are available in the traditional stilt houses which are set in charming gardens with various Asian-style fountains and infinity pools. The spa is equipped with a heated indoor pool and hot tub, and there is also a fitness centre.
